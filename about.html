About Us

Who We Are

Dream Sweet Bouquet is a floral design studio based in Nairobi, Kenya. Since our founding, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting heartfelt, vibrant floral arrangements for every moment in life—from joyful celebrations to tender goodbyes.

Our team believes in more than just flowers—we believe in emotion, in storytelling, and in the magic that a fresh bouquet can bring. We work closely with local growers to ensure sustainability and freshness, and we deliver beauty right to your doorstep.

Whether you're planning your dream wedding, surprising a loved one, or just treating yourself, Dream Sweet Bouquet brings your vision to life—petal by petal.
